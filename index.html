Odin Recipes - Cooking the Perfect Peach

There are four movements that target the glute muscles:

  • thrust
  • hinge
  • squat
  • abduct

Each movement targets the glutes or a certain area of the glutes, but may also engage other muscles (hammies and quads). Because I am quad-dominant, I tend to grow my thighs more than my glute. To solve this, I've worked on finding workouts that combine these movements in a way to target my glutes more.

Note: For all exercises, make sure that your core is tight, you are thinking about your butt flexing with each movement (adjust your position/speed/weights as needed), and that you are breathing.
